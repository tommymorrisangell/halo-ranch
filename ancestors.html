TOM & CHRIS ANGELL SPRING FAMILY REUNION 2018

 

 I would like the entire family to form a lasting mental picture of the four Solomon Angells pictured here! The list starts with Solomon (1806) a member of the very first, First Quorum of the Seventy, and ending with Solomon (1925) the greatest Dad that ever lived.



  Hi Everybody

Only Jerry's family spoke for specific relatives for this family reunion and I've chosen the ancestors for the rest of the grandkids. I've only chose ancestors for my grandkids who are old enough to do their own research. However, parents, if you have a younger child that truly wants to participate, choose a relative for them and go for it. This website can be used as a source of information, but by all means research your chosen relative in many other places.  I would like the grandkids to tell all about the person that they have been assigned to in first-person and be ready to answer questions about that person as if they were that person.  Please have at least one picture and be prepared to tell how he or she fits in our family.  Please research things like birth dates, occupations, special deeds, places of birth, places that they have lived and many, many more. Your goal will be to enlighten the rest of the family concerning the person you represent.

For those of you with recently deceased relatives, you will need to call and  interview people who have known them.


Please take this project serious and be prepared to participate at the Family Reunion.
