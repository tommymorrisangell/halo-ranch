        ANCESTORS OF THE TOM AND CHRIS ANGELL FAMILY

    I  want my family to take genealogy real serious.  I want them to appreciate what our ancestors have went through in order for us to be here today. Each year at our annual spring family reunion we have an evening set apart for the older grandkids to share what they have learned about their assigned or chosen ancestor and do it as if they were that person. I want them to to do enough research  that they will feel comfortable telling about the person that they represent and answer questions about them as well.  This web page will serve as a helper in doing the research.  When the reunion is over, I would like the grandkids to share with me the information that they have found and I will add it to this web page for future reference.
